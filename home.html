ABOUT US

Welcome to Inner Compass! We're not just a web application; we're your trusted companion on your mental health journey. With us, you can connect with licensed professionals, dive into supportive peer communities, and explore a wealth of educational resources—all tailored to your needs. Our platform is designed to be safe, inclusive, and easy to navigate, ensuring that everyone feels welcome and supported. Let's navigate this journey together, breaking down barriers and prioritizing mental well-being, one step at a time. Join us, and let's embark on this empowering adventure together!


SERVICES

  • Confidential Assessments: Take control of your mental well-being with our confidential assessment tool. Monitor your progress and gain insights into your mental health journey.
  • Online Therapy Sessions: Connect with qualified therapists directly through our platform for personalized online therapy sessions. Additionally, access a library of recorded therapy videos for continuous support whenever you need it.
  • Community Connect: Step into our supportive community where you can freely interact with others facing similar challenges. Whether you're on the path to recovery or simply seeking understanding, our platform provides a safe space for meaningful connections, supported by chatbots for added comfort and anonymity.
  • Self-Help Resources: Dive into a wealth of curated content including soothing music, self-help videos, art therapy, and mindfulness practices. We help you discover the perfect resources to support your mental well-being journey.
  • Hope Fortune Cookie: Receive daily doses of positivity and inspiration with our Hope Fortune Cookie feature. Store your favorite affirmations and quotes to uplift your spirits whenever you need a boost.
  • Crisis Intervention Support: Help is just a click away during times of acute distress or when dealing with suicidal thoughts. Our professional support team are here to provide immediate assistance and guidance.
  • Virtual Pet Companions: Nurture and interact with adorable virtual pet companions to alleviate stress and loneliness. These virtual buddies offer companionship and comfort, making your mental health journey a little brighter.

CLIENTS

At Inner Compass, we cater to individuals from all walks of life who are committed to prioritizing their mental health and well-being. Our target audience includes:

  • Individuals looking for convenient and accessible access to qualified therapists for personalized support and guidance.

  • Individuals experiencing acute distress or seeking immediate assistance during moments of crisis, including those dealing with suicidal thoughts.
  • Individuals interested in exploring and utilizing curated resources for mindfulness, personal growth, and self-improvement.
